- Pixel Fonts:
You probably guessed it by now, making Pixel Art is something I fairly enjoy. But good Pixel Art equals good Pixel Fonts! And considering the fact that my pixel art isn't that great, I need even better fonts to make it shine!
Those fonts were made through Fontstruct.com, and its "no dark mode policy" which killed my eyes.
Abject New Roman:
Pun intended. A font with all the weird letters and symbols you could dream (except if you write with something else than the derivatives of the Latin alphabet...).
Abject Long:
Old version of Abject New Roman, but was too restrictive considering its looong capital letters. Still a fun font with a lot of symbols and a unic aesthetic.
